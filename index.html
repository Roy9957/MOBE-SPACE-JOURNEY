The Community

Development Timeline

2018 - Concept

Initial ideas and prototype development began with a small team of 5 developers.

2019 - Alpha

First playable version released to select testers. Core gameplay mechanics established.

2020 - Beta

Public beta launched with 50,000 active players. Major systems refined based on feedback.

2021 - Launch

Full version released worldwide to critical acclaim. Over 1 million players in first month.
